Gros Gary Collection is born from the wish to create a different brand.
An inclusive brand for which all types of bodies and shapes matter.
An ethical brand not only offering the basics.
A brand based on a crafts(wo)manship allowing the creation of durable clothing for all.

Gros Gary Collection is therefore my way to be an ally to two fights.
A fight for a fashion industry which includes and represents all women.
A fight for a fashion industry that produces responsably on both social and ecological levels.

Each piece of clothing is available in sizes going from XXS to 5XL. A personnalised option is offered for any needed adjustements or if you feel left out from our size chart.

Raw materials are carefully selected to offer both quality and lowest environmental impact. The cotton is organic and GOTS certified. The ink is free of any harmful substances (Oeko-Tex Standard 100). The leather is vegan. Small supplies are thrifted as much as possible.

The collection is entirely handmade in a workshop in Toulouse, France. Each step of the making process is carefully executed to deliver clothes that fit you the best and that you can keep for a long time !

Anna Buros Founder of Gros Gary Collection.
